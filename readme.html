The Phone Call

The infamous phone call that took place on July 25, 2019 between 9:03 - 9:33 am EDT between the US President Donald J. Trump and the newly elected President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy. After revelations from a whistle blower, a memorandum of the phone call was released on September 24, 2019. This phone call led to Donald J. Trump to become the third US President to be impeached.

This repository contains a - text version of the memorandum that was obtained by using OCR - cleaned version of the text memorandum (no edits to the contents of the phone call were made, I only cleaned up artifacts from the conversion process and moved non-phone call related text to spots that I felt made sense) - script used to obtain the text version of the memorandum

The reason that I used OCR to pull the text from the PDF is that the text in the document didn‚Äôt copy and paste very well and the results from the OCR were much better.
